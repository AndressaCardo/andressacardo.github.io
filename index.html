About Me

My name is Andressa, and I am a meteorologist from Brazil. I hold a master's degree and a PhD in Atmospheric Science from the University of São Paulo, Brazil. My background includes research on cyclones, climatology, climate, and prediction models. Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Meteorology at the University of Reading in the United Kingdom. My research focuses on Mediterranean cyclones, diabatic processes, and numerical weather prediction models. I am passionate about weather, particularly weather forecasts. On this website, I intend to share my professional background, which includes my publications and current projects, as well as my interest in weather and climate for a non-expert audience. This is my personal website, and I am excited to showcase my hobbies and activities related to weather, particularly my passion for photography, cuisine, museums and nature.
