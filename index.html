About Me

My name is Andressa, I am from Brazil and meteorologist. I have a master and a PhD degree in Atmospheric Science from the University of São Paulo, Brazil. I have a background about cyclones, climatatology, climate and prediction models. Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Meteorology, University of Reading (United Kingdom), and my research focuses on Mediterranean cyclones, diabatic processes and Numerical Weather Prediction models. I am passionate about weather things, specially related to forecast. In this website, I aimed to add my professional information as Publications and conferenced I attended, as well as curiosity about weather and climate for a non meteorologist people. Additionally, since it is a personal website, I will add some hobbies activities, such as photography, which some of them related to weather.
